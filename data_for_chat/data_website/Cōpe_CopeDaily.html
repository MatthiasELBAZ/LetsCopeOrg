FAQ

Resilience is a learned skill

You can’t learn everything in one hour, so our coaches will arm you with suggested science-based practices and teachings that help you become more resilient to do daily between cōpe coach sessions.

Why are daily practices needed?

Research tells us that if we want to create new connections and enhance resilience, three things are required:

Intention

Action

Repetition

cōpe daily activities embody each of these requirements and help you better cope with a chronic illness or life-altering diagnosis by:

Maintaining structure and routine

Prioritizing a daily practice or routine gives you control that a diagnosis often takes away.

Building resilience

 To cope, is to be resilient - by becoming more resilient, the better we can cope with life’s adversities.

Boosting mental health

Depression and anxiety often accompany a chronic or inflammatory illness, cōpe practices help you stay ahead of stressors.

Finding purpose and meaning

Align actions with your values and goals to promote personal growth.

Access anytime, any device.

Our programs, classes, and articles are available 24/7 on any device that has internet connectivity.

Programs and practices to support your mental health and well-being

We are tailoring your journey. Personalized programs and practices.

Self-Love Challenge

cōpe experts

This challenge is rooted in compassion, mindfulness, and a profound respect for the resilience of the human spirit. We understand that the challenges you face may encompass physical, emotional, and psychological hurdles. That's why it combines the wisdom of trauma-informed principles with the profound healing potential of self-love practices.

Navigating Challenges

cōpe experts

In this series, you will learn simple practices that can be used any time to navigate the challenges you face in your life

Healing Reset

cōpe experts

If you're not happy with your current health circumstances, this 3-day challenge will help you remove things that don't serve you and add things that do to ease your path. Using epienergetics, Talia leads you through exercises you can use to support you as you cope with a life-limiting health challenge.

Mindfulness Meditation

cōpe experts

Have you tried meditation and thought that it just wasn't for you? We consider mediation a practice, simply because it takes practice! This series will guide you through the hard parts so you can drop into the stillness. Even if you're a seasoned meditator, dive deeper with Cyndi Lee to be more mindful in your practice.

Beginner Foodie

cōpe experts

A program for those looking to develop new habits when it comes to food and nutrition. Chef Perla has created an amazing program that will explore the healing power of foods, how to read labels, the idea of batch cooking and more. You'll put her tips into practice in the grocery store, your pantry, and by creating several delicious meals to fuel your body.

Intentional Laughter

cōpe experts

Through life you may have been taught to laugh only at things that are "funny". This limits our chances for a good belly laugh, as humor is subjective and doesn't allow us more than a few seconds of laughter at a time. Intentional laughter (used in both Laughter Yoga and Laughter Wellness) is about choosing to allow laughter, which in itself is an empowering act that can lead to choice, allowing and empowerment in other areas.
